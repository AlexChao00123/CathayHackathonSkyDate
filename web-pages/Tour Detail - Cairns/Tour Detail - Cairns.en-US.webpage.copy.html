​4-day Diving Trip in Great Barrier Reefs!!

​Country: Cairns, Australia
Features: Diving, eco-tour, water sports and more!
Budget: $8000-$10000
Preferred language: English
Participant preference: 25-35yo (15 people)
Duration: 4 days (4 Jan - 8 Jan)
Category: sports, eco-toursim

Day 1:
Arrival in Cairns, Australia
→ Transfer to the hotel
→ Check-in at hotel
→ Orientation session with diving instructors and overview of the diving itinerary
→ Optional evening stroll along the Cairns Esplanade and dinner at a local seafood restaurant

Day 2:
Breakfast at Hotel
→ Full day diving excursion to the Great Barrier Reef
→ Enjoy a packed lunch
→ Optional snorkeling sessions for non-divers to also experience the beauty of the reef
→ Return to Cairns in the late afternoon
→ Evening free to relax or explore the city's nightlife, with options for live music or a sunset cruise

​Day 3:
Breakfast at Hotel
→ Participate in an eco-tour
→ Engage in hands-on activities - coral planting → Lunch at a scenic spot on one of the reef islands
→ Afternoon water sports activities such as kayaking or paddleboarding
→ Sunset beach BBQ dinner with a bonfire

​Day 4:
Morning dive session to explore a different section of the reef
→ Encounter more marine life and perhaps spot sea turtles, rays, and tropical fish
→ Return to the dive resort and freshen up
→ Visit the Cairns Aquarium for a closer look at the reef's marine species
→ Enjoy a farewell dinner near the promenade
→ Travel to Caims Airport
