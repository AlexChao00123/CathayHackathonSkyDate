Hokkaido Skiing Tour (5-day tour)

​Country: Hokkaido, Japan
Features: Skiing, eating, hot spring and more!
Budget: $5000-7000
Preferred language: Chinese
Participant preference: 20-30yo skiing enthusiast (20 people)
Duration: 5 days (Mid-December)
Category: entertainment, experience 

​Day 1:
 Arrival in Hokkaido, Japan (CX580)
→ Transfer to the ski resort
→ Check-in at the hotel
→ Explore the surroundings
→ Dinner at a local restaurant

​Day 2:
Breakfast at Hotel
→ Full day of skiing on the slopes of the ski resort
→ Take ski lessons if desired or explore the intermediate and advanced runs
→ Japanese lunch at a mountain restaurant
→ Relax and rejuvenate in a hot spring (onsen)
→ Dinner at a local izakaya, a traditional Japanese pub

​Day 3:
Breakfast at Hotel
→ Morning skiing session
→ Lunch at a cosy café with a view of the mountains
→ Afternoon visit to a local sake brewery for a tasting experience
→ Explore the nearby town and try some local street food → Dinner at a traditional Japanese restaurant specialising in seafood

Day 4:
Breakfast at Hotel
→ Full day Skiing Session
→ Skiing on different slopes and exploring new trails  → Lunch at a mountain hut
→ Visit a local artisan craft shop to see traditional Japanese crafts
→ Dinner at Ramen Local Shop

​Day 5:
Breakfast at Local Restaurant
→ Final morning ski session to make the most of the remaining time on the slopes
→ Visit a local market to sample and purchase Hokkaido's famous dairy products and fresh seafood
→ Transfer to the airport for departure
→ Onboard CX581
