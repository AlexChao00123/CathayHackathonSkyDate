Frequently Asked Questions

Welcome to TourMatch! Below are some of the most common questions we receive about our tour matching app.

1

How do I create a tour?

Creating a tour is easy! Simply log in to your account and click on the 'Create Tour' button. From there, you can select your destination, dates, and preferences to find the perfect tour for you.

2

How do I search for tours?

You can search for tours by clicking on the 'Search Tours' button on the homepage. From there, you can enter your destination, dates, and preferences to find the perfect tour for you.

3

How do I book a tour?

Booking a tour is easy! Simply select the tour you want to book and click on the 'Book Now' button. From there, you can enter your payment information and confirm your booking.

4

What if I need to cancel my tour?

If you need to cancel your tour, please contact our customer support team as soon as possible. Depending on the cancellation policy of the tour provider, you may be eligible for a refund.

5

How do I contact customer support?

You can contact our customer support team by clicking on the 'Contact Us' button on the homepage. From there, you can fill out a form with your question or concern, and we will get back to you as soon as possible.

6

How do I update my account information?

You can update your account information by clicking on the 'My Account' button on the homepage. From there, you can edit your profile, change your password, and update your payment information.
